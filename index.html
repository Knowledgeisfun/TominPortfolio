TOMIN JAMES

I am a multidisciplinary creative director specializing in visual media. My work explores the intersection of raw human expression and polished technical precision. I believe the most powerful stories are told when artistry and technology converge.

From crafting garment collections to directing cinematic shorts, my focus is on building immersive worlds that resonate. This portfolio is a curated look into my process and passion across fashion, photography, and film.

Process

AURA Collection

DISCIPLINE: Fashion Design

ROLE: Creative Director

GOAL: To create a lookbook exploring ethereal light and shadow.

The Inspiration

The AURA collection began with a fascination for the transient nature of light. The core challenge was to translate this intangible concept into tangible garments that felt both structured and weightless.

Urban Portraits

DISCIPLINE: Photography

ROLE: Lead Photographer

GOAL: To capture the authentic character of the city through its people.

The Vision

This series aimed to move beyond posed shots and capture candid, authentic moments. The city itself became a secondary character, its textures and light framing the subjects' stories.

Kinetic Titles

DISCIPLINE: Videography / Motion

ROLE: Director & Animator

GOAL: To design a high-energy title sequence for a fictional tech conference.

The Concept

The goal was to create a visual identity in motion that felt both futuristic and human. Using a combination of fast-paced typography, abstract shapes, and glitch effects, the sequence builds energy and excitement.
